Senior Backend Engineer — Payments Platform

About the role
--------------
We're hiring a senior backend engineer to join our payments platform team.
You'll own service design and implementation for core payment flows (auth,
capture, refund, reconciliation) used by thousands of merchants, and work
closely with our risk, ledger, and integrations teams.

Required
--------
- 5+ years of professional backend engineering experience, ideally building
  transactional systems (payments, banking, ledger, billing, or similar).
- Strong Python OR Go in production. Comfortable designing, reviewing, and
  operating services written in your primary language.
- Solid working knowledge of PostgreSQL: schema design, indexes, transaction
  isolation, query tuning.
- Experience building and maintaining REST or gRPC APIs that other teams
  depend on.
- Production operational maturity: you've owned services, run incidents, and
  written postmortems.
- Familiar with distributed-systems fundamentals: idempotency, retries,
  at-least-once delivery, eventual consistency, circuit breakers.

Nice to have
------------
- Direct experience with payment rails (Stripe, Adyen, ACH, card networks).
- Event streaming (Kafka, Kinesis, Pub/Sub).
- Kubernetes, Terraform, and general infrastructure-as-code comfort.
- Exposure to compliance regimes (PCI DSS, SOC 2).
- Experience mentoring mid-level engineers.

You will
--------
- Design and implement new payment flows end-to-end, from schema to API to
  rollout.
- Improve observability, reliability, and cost of the existing platform.
- Participate in on-call rotation (roughly one week every six).
- Raise the quality bar through code review, design review, and mentorship.
